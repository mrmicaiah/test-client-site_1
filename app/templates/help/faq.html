PROSPECTS & CLIENTS

What's the difference between a prospect and a client? ▼

A prospect is someone who's interested but hasn't accepted an estimate yet. Once they accept your estimate, they automatically become a client.

How do I convert a prospect to a client? ▼

It happens automatically when they accept your estimate. You can also manually change their status by editing the client and changing "Status" to "Client."

ESTIMATES

How does the client accept an estimate? ▼

When you send an estimate, they receive a link. They click it, see the estimate details, and tap "Accept." You'll see the status change in the app.

Can I edit an estimate after sending it? ▼

Yes, you can edit estimates that are in "Draft" or "Sent" status. Once accepted, you can't edit it anymore.

What's the difference between weekly, biweekly, and monthly? ▼

Weekly = every week (4-5 visits/month)
Biweekly = every 2 weeks (2 visits/month)
Monthly = once a month
One-time = single visit, no recurring schedule

SCHEDULING

How does the 8-week rolling schedule work? ▼

When you add a client to the schedule, we create 8 weeks of visits automatically. As you complete visits, new ones are added to keep 8 weeks scheduled ahead. You never have to manually add recurring visits.

How do I reschedule a visit? ▼

Tap on the visit, then tap "Reschedule." Pick a new date and time.

How do I stop service for a client? ▼

Edit the client and change their status to "Inactive." You'll have the option to cancel all future visits.

INVOICING

When should I invoice? ▼

Most cleaners invoice at the end of each month. Go to the client's page and tap "Invoice for Month" to see all completed visits and create an invoice.

Can I add notes to visits that show on the invoice? ▼

Yes! When marking a visit complete, choose "Complete with Notes" to add details like "Deep cleaned oven" or "Extra bathroom." These notes appear on the invoice.

How do I get paid? ▼

MiKlean doesn't process payments directly. Set up your payment info (Venmo, Zelle, check, etc.) in Settings, and it will appear on your invoices. When the client pays you, mark the invoice as "Paid."
